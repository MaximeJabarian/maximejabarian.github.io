About me

Hi, I'm Maxime. I'm a Data Scientist / AI engineer. I hold two Master’s degree in science (Artificial Intelligence and Astrophysics, at Lyon Observatory and Centrale Supélec). After having worked for renowned research institutes such as the University of Cambridge and private companies like Alltricks and Entrepreneur First, I'm now looking for a new Data Scientist position. I thrive on transforming raw data into meaningful and actionable insights. I meticulously analyze and piece together data to reveal a story that can empower businesses to make informed decisions and drive success. From predictive modeling to deploying machine learning solutions, my expertise in data science has helped businesses across industries unlock the full potential of their data.
Do you want to work together? Please reach out to me by e-mail.

I translate data into valuable and comprehensible insights using machine learning models. My goal is to improve results and save costs.

I am experienced in machine learning, supervised and unsupervised algorithms and use data visualization techniques to present the results. I use Python, SQL, penthao and Power BI for my data projects.
